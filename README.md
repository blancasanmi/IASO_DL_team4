# IASO_DL_team4
We built deep learning models to predict biomass quantity from the kaggle competition https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/csiro-biomass/data 

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) notebook 
- all the members of the group did their own EDA and we grouped some of the most important things in this condensed notebook.
- this task was really important because we all managed to get an overall idea of the project and we looked in depth at different aspects of the data

## Preprocessing notebook 
- two members of the group worked on the preprocessing. 
- the idea was to make a common way of preprocessing including handling the image transformations and train-test splits
- everything was saved in a common drive folder so that we could all refer to that without needing to rerun transformations
- the only thing all members had to re-run was the normalization of the pictures since we decided not to save the pictures normalized

## Model notebooks (2)
- we decided to work on two models: one that only looked at the images, and another that looked at images AND tabular data
- we subdivided into two groups of three in which every member had to run the models and hyperparameter tuning, from there the subgroups decided on a common best model strategy and evaluated them on the test data

## Evaluation notebook 
- this last notebook was made to capture all the information from the evaluation on the different models and plot and analyse all the relevant things

## Bonus
- striking to always achieve more, one of our members also trained a visual transformer (ViT). Our aim was to see what were the main differences in the results, compared to a CNN
